What is NASA's full name?

NASA stands for the National Aeronautics and Space Administration. It is the independent U.S. federal agency responsible for civilian space exploration, aeronautics research, and the aerospace program.

What did NASA stand for?

Established by the U.S. government in 1958, it is the independent civilian agency responsible for the nation's science and technology related to air and space travel.
